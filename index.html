BMW E30

The BMW E30 is the second generation of the BMW 3 Series, manufactured from 1982 to 1994, and succeeded E21 3 Series. The model range included 2-door coupe and convertible body styles, as well as the first 3-Series 4-door sedan and wagon/estate body styles. It was powered by four-cylinder gasoline, six-cylinder gasoline, and six-cylinder diesel engines, the latter of which was a first for the 3 Series.The BMW E30 325iX was the first to feature all-wheel drive.

Transmission

The power is transferred to the road via rear wheel drive (RWD) and a 5 speed manual gearbox.

Subaru WRX STI

The Subaru WRX is an all-wheel drive sport compact car produced by Subaru. It is based on the Impreza, which was designed for the World Rally Championship in 1992. Subaru claims the WRX actually stands for "World Rally eXperimental." The WRX lineup has been separated from the Impreza lineup since 2014, with a different body style that is not available as an optional hatchback/wagon.

Engine Specs

The WRX STI retais the previous generation STI's EJ engine (2.0-litre EJ207 for Japanese models, 2.5-litre EJ257 for international models), as well as dual AVCS. It does, however, have ECU modifications that have increased fuel economy and provided a slight power boost. The EJ257 engine continues to be quoted at 305 hp (227 kW), but with 290 lbft (393 Nm) of torque, a slight decrease from the previous generation, but this is thought to be due to ECU reprogramming to support a stronger top end, and true power output is estimated to be around 235-240 kW. The EJ207 has a power output of 300 hp (224 kW) and a torque output of 311 lbft (422 Nm).The WRX STI also retains the previous generation's 6-speed TY85 manual transmission (the CVT is not available on the STI).

Transmission

The WRX now comes standard with a 6-speed TY75 manual transmission, up from 5-speeds. It is also available with a Lineartronic TR690 CVT with 6 or 8 simulated "gears" accessible via steering-wheel paddle shifters. The CVT is the only transmission option for the Japanese-spec WRX S4.

Subaru BRZ

The Subaru BRZ is a coupe manufactured by Subaru in collaboration with Toyota, which manufactures its GT86 technical twin at the Subaru plant in Gunma, Japan. It is the result of two concept cars: the BRZ Prologue, which debuted at the Frankfurt Motor Show in September 2011, and the BRZ STi Concept, which debuted at the Los Angeles Motor Show in November 2011.

Engine Specs

The BRZ breaks with Subaru's tradition of four-wheel drive and innovates propulsion. Its engine architecture however remains typically Subaru with its atmospheric 2.0 4-cylinder flat developing 200 hp and 205 N m. The low centre of gravity allows a very good weight distribution for this coupe: 53% at the front and 47% at the rear.

Audi Quattro

The Audi Quattro is a road and rally car manufactured by Audi, a division of the Volkswagen Group. On March 3, 1980, it made its debut at the Geneva Motor Show. Production lasted until 1991.

Engine Specs

The original engine was a 2,144 cc (2.1 L) inline-5-cylinder 10-valve SOHC with a turbocharger and intercooler. It produced 147 kW (200 PS; 197 hp) and 285 Nm (210 lbft) of torque at 3,500 rpm, propelling the Quattro from 0 to 100 km/h (62 mph) in 7.1 seconds and on to a top speed of over 220 km/h (137 mph)

Mercedes-AMG revealed the Roadster (internal designation R 190) for the first time on September 15, 2016. This also made its public debut at the Paris Motor Show. The four-door variant (internal designation X 290) was revealed at the 2018 Geneva Motor Show and has been on the market since September 2018.

Engine Specs

The M 178 engine is a V8 engine with a displacement of 4 liters which is equipped behind the front axle as a front mid-engine. The very first AMG GT's maximum engine output is 340 kW (462 hp) at 6000 rpm. The engine weighs 209 kilograms and has a maximum torque of 600 Newton meters. The engine in the GT S produces 375 kW (510 hp) with a maximum torque of 650 Nm, while the GT R, which debuted at the Goodwood Festival of Speed in June 2016, produces 430 kW (585 hp) and 700 Nm. The M178 engine is also used in the roadster. Aside from the GT, a new GT C model with 410 kW (557 hp) or 680 Nm debuted.

Transmission

The engine sends power to the wheels through a 7-speed automatic transmission.
